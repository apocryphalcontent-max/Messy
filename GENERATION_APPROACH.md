# Master Taxonomy Generation Approach

## Objective
Generate thousands of profound intellectual subjects for each of the 8 categories of the Master Taxonomy of the Opus Maximus, with NO PLACEHOLDERS.

## Quality Standards
- **Profound**: Each subject addresses deep philosophical questions
- **Intellectual**: Drawn from the canon of Western and Eastern thought
- **Beautiful**: Named with care and precision
- **Commanding Orthodox Perspectives**: Grounded in traditional wisdom while engaging modern questions
- **Earned Sadness**: Acknowledging the limits of human knowledge while pursuing truth
